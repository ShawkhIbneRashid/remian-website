About Remians Canada

Remians are the graduates of Dhaka Residential Model College, Dhaka, Bangladesh. Remians Canada was formed by the Alumni of Dhaka Residential Model College. This group is a hub for the Remians living in Canada for their welfare and to maintain a strong bond among them. If you are a Remian and currently in Canada become a memeber. We also have a Facebook page which you can check out to learn more about our activitites.
